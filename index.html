Director Statement

25 years of bringing
characters to life.

I've spent 25 years inside the frame — first as a 2D animator in Chennai, learning to give weight and soul to drawn characters, and eventually as the person responsible for the vision that holds an entire production together.

From Technicolor India to Brown Bag Films in Dublin, from studios in Indonesia to Redefine DNEG in Hyderabad — I've directed animation for Netflix, Disney, DreamWorks and Sega. The scale changes. The question never does: does this character feel alive?

Currently Animation Director at Redefine DNEG, Hyderabad. Available for international projects and overseas supervision roles.
